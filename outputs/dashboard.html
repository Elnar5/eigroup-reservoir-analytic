Reservoir Analytics Dashboard

eiGroup Associate Data Scientist assessment · 7 wells · 5 zones · 18,167 samples · 105 tests · Kamil Muradli, May 2026
Headline findings:
  • Zone B is the dominant flow interval (kh ≈ 10.7 M mD·m, NTG 93%) — but 88% of samples are at the 15,000 mD tool cap, so its kh is a conservative lower bound, not a best estimate.
  • Zone D is tight rock: NTG never exceeds 32% even at the loosest vsh cutoff. Bypass it.
  • Zone C splits into 3 reproducible sub-zones across all 7 wells. Sub-zone 2 holds 48% of Zone C kh in just 29% of its thickness — natural drilling target.
  • Well-to-well kh ranking is partly an instrument artefact: well 7 leads kh but has the highest saturation fraction (30%).
